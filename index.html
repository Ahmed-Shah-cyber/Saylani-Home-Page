SAYLANI SERVICES

SAYLANI
HEALTHCARE

Bridging the needs of quality healthcare is a top priority for us. The goal of Saylani Health Care Services is to build a healthy society and outreach medically underserved and neglected communities not only through provision of high quality free of cost health services & supplies but also by acting patients and health institutions.

SAYLANI
MASS TRAINING

Saylani Mass IT Training Program. To Train 10 Thousand Developers in Pakistan. We want to empower our youth to think, create and change the software world. To achieve this goal, Saylani launched its Saylani Mass I.T (SMIT) Program. We have collaborated with world-renowned organizations for SMIT that include Microsoft, CISCO, SDC, Viftech and City Guild UK.

SAYLANI
DASTARKWAN

We are pleased to say that more than 100,000 people are being fed across Pakistan daily. Each person is served with respect and dignity and it is our highest honor to serve them. With our service, they know that there is always a place where they can eat 365 days a year, and no one needs to sleep hungry at night.
